# tone9 handoff — 2026-06-18

## What tone9 is

`t9` is the new repo/runtime for generating compact Orthodox weekly service
outlines. It keeps the user's ODT/PDF workflow but moves the source of truth into
registries, service sidecars, fixtures, and generator code.

## Important style/liturgical rules

- Do not use exact bare `var-incipit`.
- Totals such as `10+2 total`, `8+2 total`, and `6 total` are underlined, not bold.
- Kanon count numerals are spoken/read counts and are not bold.
- Bold line-initial counts only for paired sung/read stichera/stichoi structures.
- Preserve full incipits; solve fit with tabs/layout, not clipping.
- ODS service marker is a derived/review field, not the Matins Gospel section.
- Matins Gospel cycle drives Gospel incipit, Exapostilarion, Theotokion, and
  Evangelical Sticheron.
- God-is-the-Lord Theotokion tone follows the preceding verse.
- Optional slots are governed by service shape, not by deleting rows from every
  boilerplate template.
